Is it legal to remove password protection from PDF files?

It is legal to unlock PDF files that you own or have permission to modify. However, it is illegal to unlock PDFs that you do not own or have authorization to access. Our tool is intended for legitimate personal and professional use only.

What types of security can this tool remove?

Our PDF Unlocker can remove password protection, as well as restrictions on printing, copying text, editing, and document assembly. It works for both user passwords (required to open the file) and owner passwords (restricting permissions).

Will my PDF file quality be affected?

No, our unlocking process doesn't alter the content or quality of your PDF file. The resulting unlocked PDF will be identical to the original in terms of content and quality.

What is the maximum file size I can unlock?

You can unlock PDF files up to 50MB in size. For larger files, we recommend using our desktop software version (coming soon).

Is there a limit on how many files I can unlock?

No, you can unlock as many files as you need. There are no usage limits on our free tool.
